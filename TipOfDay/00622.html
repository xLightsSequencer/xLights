Audio Reactive Shaders

Some shaders can respond to your music while they run, so the visuals pulse and shift in time with the song instead of just looping on their own.

In the Shader effect settings you can choose what drives the reaction. A shader can feed off the overall intensity of the audio, or off the frequency information from a VU meter style analysis, depending on which the shader supports.

This is an easy way to get a beat-driven background without hand-placing effects to every note. Drop a reactive shader across a matrix and it will dance along with whatever audio your sequence is using.
