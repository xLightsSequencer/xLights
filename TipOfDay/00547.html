Emojis In The Shape Effect

The Shape effect can draw emojis as well as its built-in shapes. There is a popup list of emojis to choose from, including seasonal favourites, and you can pick a skin tone for the ones that support it.

Emojis render in their native multi-colour form, so a single Shape effect can scatter little coloured icons across a matrix or tree. It is a fun, fast way to add recognisable imagery without drawing anything yourself.

Try combining emoji shapes with the effect's movement and sizing settings to make them drift, grow, or bounce across your display.
