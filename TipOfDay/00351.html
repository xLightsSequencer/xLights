Resize Image Props

Image type props on the Layout tab let you edit their dimensions directly. Rather than being stuck with the size the image came in at, you can set the width and height you want for the prop in its properties.

This is useful when you use a picture as a non-lit backdrop or decoration in your preview. Sizing it correctly makes your layout match the real-world scene more closely.

A well-sized image prop helps you judge spacing and placement of your lit models around it, so your virtual preview lines up with what people will actually see in the yard.
