Add A Terrain Object

A Terrain object is a non-lit layout object that draws a ground surface in your preview, helping you visualise where your display sits relative to the landscape.

On the Layout tab you can add a Terrain object from the objects you place into the scene, then size and position it like any other model or object. It does not control any lights of its own; it is there to give your preview a sense of place.

This is handy when you are laying out a yard display in 3D and want a reference for the ground, slopes, or where props rest. It makes the preview feel closer to the real scene as you arrange everything.
