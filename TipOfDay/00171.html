Subdivide Timing Tracks

You can generate a new timing track that splits each of your existing timing marks into smaller, evenly spaced sections.

Right-click on a timing track and look for the option to create a subdivided timing track. xLights takes every gap between existing marks and divides it into the number of pieces you choose, producing a finer track without you placing each mark by hand.

This is handy when you have beat marks already laid down and want eighth notes, triplets, or any tighter grid to sequence against. You keep your original track and get a denser one to work from alongside it.
