Spotting Vertical Single Lines

Perfectly vertical single line models are now highlighted in the layout, the same way perfectly horizontal lines already were.

When you draw or nudge a single line model and it lines up exactly vertical, xLights shows you, so you can tell at a glance that it is truly straight rather than just close. Check Sequence will also warn you about single lines that have been reversed.

This makes it much easier to keep rows of vertical elements neat and consistent without zooming in to second-guess every model.
