Drag Rows To Reorder Models And Timing Tracks

You no longer need to dig into Display Elements just to move a model or timing track up or down in the sequencer.

Grab a row by its header on the left of the grid and drag it up or down to wherever you want it. This works for both model rows and timing track rows.

It's a small thing, but if you reorganize your sequencer layout often, this saves a lot of trips to a separate dialog.
