Scale A Model Group

On the layout tab you can select a model group and resize everything in it at once. Grab the handles around the group's selection box and drag to scale all of its models together.

The models keep their positions relative to each other as the group grows or shrinks, so the overall arrangement stays intact. This saves you from nudging each model individually when you need to make a section bigger or fit it into a new space.

It is a quick way to adjust a whole prop cluster, a mega tree, or a matrix of models after you have measured your real display and need the layout to match.
