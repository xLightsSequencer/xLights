Eleven Labs Lyric Import

xLights can now import lyrics using Eleven Labs, giving you another option for turning a song's words into timing tracks.

You reach it from the lyric import workflow when you create timing marks from your audio. It analyzes the track and brings in the words so you have phrase, word and phoneme timings ready to sequence against.

Accurate lyric timing is the hard part of building a singing-face or text display. Letting the service do the first pass gives you a solid starting point that you can fine-tune instead of marking every word by hand.
