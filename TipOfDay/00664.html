See Which Groups A Model Belongs To

The model properties show the groups that a model, or any of its submodels, belongs to.

Select a model on the Layout tab and look through its properties to find the list of groups it is part of. This works for submodels too, so you can see exactly where a piece of a model is being used.

It takes the guesswork out of group membership when you have many overlapping groups. Before you change or delete something, you can quickly check what else relies on it.
