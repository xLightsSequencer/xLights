Italic And Slanted Text

The Text effect can now render fonts in italic and slant styles.

When you pick a font for a Text effect, you can choose an italic or slanted variant in addition to the usual upright styles. The leaning letters give your words a different feel without you having to find and install a separate font.

It's a nice touch for song titles, names, or any message where a bit of style helps the text stand out on your display.
