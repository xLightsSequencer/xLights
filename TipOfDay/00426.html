Warm Up Your Snowflakes And Meteors

The Snowflakes and Meteors effects have a warmup frames setting that lets the effect build up before the part you see begins.

You will find it in the effect's settings. Increasing the warmup count runs the simulation for a number of frames before your effect starts, so the screen is already full of falling snow or streaking meteors at the very first frame.

Without warmup these effects start empty and gradually fill in. Adding warmup frames means the motion looks established right away, which is especially nice for short effects that would otherwise end before they got going.
