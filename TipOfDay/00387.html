WebP Image Support

xLights can use WebP images in effects that take a picture, such as the Pictures effect.

WebP is a modern image format that often produces smaller files than PNG or JPG at the same quality, and many images you download from the web already come in this format. You can point an effect straight at a WebP file without converting it first.

That means one less conversion step in your workflow. Drop the file in and it loads like any other image.
