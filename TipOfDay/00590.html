Upload Your Show To WLED

xLights can upload your configuration to WLED controllers. Once your controller is set up on the Controllers tab, xLights can push the output settings to it so the device matches what you have defined.

This means the pixel counts, protocol and port setup you enter in xLights get sent to the WLED device for you, rather than configuring it by hand in the WLED web interface.

Keeping the controller configured from xLights reduces mismatches between your layout and the hardware, which is one of the most common reasons pixels light up in the wrong place.
