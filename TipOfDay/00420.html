Draw Models In 3D From Any Angle

When you create a model while working in 3D mode on the Layout tab, xLights looks at the angle your view is rotated to and chooses a sensible place to start the model.

This means you can rotate to a side view and draw a model that ends up rotated ninety degrees, rather than always being laid out flat facing the camera. The model is placed near where you are looking instead of appearing far in front of or behind the view.

It makes building a 3D layout feel much more natural, because you can position yourself looking at the part of your display you want to add to and just draw it there.
