Reorder Model Aliases

You can now change the order of the aliases on a model, and clear them all at once.

In the model properties, the alias list can be reordered to suit you, and there's a way to delete every alias in one step (also available as a Lua script). Aliases are the alternate names that help xLights match a model when importing sequences from other layouts.

Keeping your aliases tidy and in a sensible order makes future imports map more reliably, which is worth doing on models that started life under a different name.
