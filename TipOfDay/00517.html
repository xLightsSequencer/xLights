Use A 3D Mouse In The Preview

If you own a 3DConnexion 3D mouse, xLights can use it to fly around the preview and layout windows.

With the device connected you can pan, orbit, and zoom your view smoothly with one hand, and even nudge individual control points, while your other hand stays on the keyboard.

For anyone building detailed 3D layouts it makes inspecting props from every angle feel natural, instead of wrestling the view with click-and-drag.
