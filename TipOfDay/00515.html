Auto Repeat Your Morph

The Morph effect has an auto repeat option that tiles the morph along the model and scales the repeat to the model's size for you.

Turn it on in the Morph settings and the single sweep becomes a run of evenly spaced repeats, without you working out the spacing by hand for each model you drop it on.

It is an easy way to get a flowing, chasing band of light across a long run of pixels, and it adapts automatically when you reuse the effect on a model of a different length.
