Preview Your Presets

The effect presets panel can show you a small preview of what a saved preset looks like, so you do not have to drop it onto a model just to remember what it does.

Browse your presets in the panel and let the preview show the motion and colours before you commit. This is especially helpful once you have built up a big library of saved looks with similar names.

Seeing the preset in action first saves a lot of trial and error, and makes it much faster to find the exact look you had in mind.
