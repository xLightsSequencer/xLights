Generate Color Palettes With ChatGPT

The AI color palette generator can now use ChatGPT as one of its service providers.

In the Generate Palette dialog you can describe the look you are after in plain words, pick ChatGPT as the provider, and let it suggest a set of colors you can drop straight into your effect.

It is a quick way to get a coordinated palette without hand-picking each color, which is especially useful when you want a themed or seasonal look but are not sure where to start.
