See Model Power Draw In The Visualiser

The controller visualizer can show the current draw for your models. As you look at how props are wired to each port, it displays an estimate of the amps each one pulls.

This appears in the visualizer on the Controllers tab, where you arrange models onto ports. Seeing the amps right next to the wiring helps you spot a port or power injection point that is being asked to carry too much.

Planning power this way, before the show is up, helps you avoid dim ends of runs and overloaded supplies, which are far harder to diagnose once everything is installed.
