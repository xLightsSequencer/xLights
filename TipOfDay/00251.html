Alternate Nodes On A Sphere

The Sphere model has an Alternate Nodes option that staggers the node placement from one row to the next instead of stacking them in straight columns.

You'll find it in the Sphere model properties on the Layout tab. Turning it on offsets every other row so the nodes fall into the gaps of the row above them.

This matches how many physical spheres are actually wired and gives a more even, rounded look across the surface, which helps your effects wrap smoothly instead of showing visible columns.
