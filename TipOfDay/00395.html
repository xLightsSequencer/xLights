Fade Piano Keys On Press

The Piano effect has an option to fade the keys as they are pressed rather than switching them on and off sharply.

Turn it on in the Piano effect settings and a pressed key eases in and out of view. This softens the look so the keyboard animation feels less abrupt as notes come and go.

Try it when a hard on-off press looks too harsh on your display. The fade gives the piano a smoother, more musical motion that follows the playing more gently.
