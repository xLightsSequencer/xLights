Import From LOR S5

xLights can import sequences from Light-O-Rama S5, reading its channel and strand data so you can bring older work across. You start an effect import and choose the LOR file as your source.

From there you map the incoming channels and props onto your own xLights models, just as you would for any other import, and the effects come in onto the models you choose.

If you are moving over from LOR or have sequences you built there, this saves you rebuilding everything by hand and gives you a head start on each song.
