Invert Channels On The DMX Effect

The DMX effect can invert individual channels, flipping their value so the low end becomes the high end and the other way around.

Look for the invert option on the channel you want in the DMX effect's settings. When it is on, a value xLights would send as low is sent high instead.

This is useful when a fixture moves or dims in the opposite direction to what you expect, letting you correct the behavior in xLights without rewiring or rebuilding the fixture definition.
