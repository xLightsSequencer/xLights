Add And Remove Models In The Visualiser

In the controller visualiser you can double-click to manage which models sit on a port. Double-click a model in the list to drop it onto a port, and double-click a model already on a port to take it off again.

When you add a model this way, the view scrolls so the port is visible, so you are not left hunting for where it landed. It is a quick alternative to dragging, especially on a busy controller with many ports.

Building up your port assignments by double-clicking keeps the whole process inside the visualiser, where you can see your wiring take shape as you go.
