New Kaleidoscope Patterns

The Kaleidoscope effect has several new patterns to choose from.

Drop a Kaleidoscope effect onto your grid and open its settings to pick from the expanded list of patterns. Each one mirrors and rotates the underlying colors in a different way, so it is worth stepping through them to see how they look on your model.

The extra patterns give you more variety without any added work, making it easy to find a fresh look for spinners, wreaths, and matrices.
