CMY Color Mixing On Moving Heads

DMX moving head models support CMY color mixing, matching fixtures that set their color with cyan, magenta, and yellow channels rather than red, green, and blue.

When you configure the moving head model, assign the CMY channels in its properties. xLights then translates the colors you sequence into the correct cyan, magenta, and yellow values for the fixture.

This means you can sequence a CMY moving head with the same color tools you use everywhere else and trust that it will display the colors you picked, without you doing the conversion by hand.
