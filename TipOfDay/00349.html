Select Effects From The Toolbar

The Select Effects dialog is available right from the sequencer toolbar. It lets you select effects across your sequence by criteria such as the effect type, rather than clicking them one at a time.

Once a set of effects is selected, you can act on all of them together, whether that is deleting, changing settings, or moving them. It turns a tedious manual hunt into a single step.

This is especially handy on busy sequences where the effects you want are scattered across many models and rows. Let the dialog gather them for you and work on the whole group at once.
