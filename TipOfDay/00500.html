The Sketch Effect

The Sketch effect lets you draw a path and have it appear to be sketched onto your model, as if an invisible pen were tracing the lines.

Drop the Sketch effect on a model and open its assistant panel to draw. You can lay down straight segments and curved paths, load a background image to trace over, and even import an SVG to use as your outline. Settings control how much of the path is drawn over the effect's duration and how it moves.

It is a great way to make logos, words, or simple line art animate on as if they were being hand-drawn, which is hard to achieve with the other effects.
