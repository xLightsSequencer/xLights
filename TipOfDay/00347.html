Choose Your Video Hardware Decoder

On Windows you can choose which video hardware decoder xLights uses when it plays back video for the Video effect. The option lives in Preferences alongside the other video settings.

Different graphics hardware works best with different decoders, so being able to pick one lets you match xLights to your machine. If videos stutter or fail to decode with one option, switching to another can clear it up.

If your videos already play smoothly there is no need to change anything, but it is good to know the choice is there when a particular clip or card gives you trouble.
