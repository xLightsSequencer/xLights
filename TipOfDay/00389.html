Gridlines Point To The Front

The layout gridlines can show an arrow that points toward the front of your display.

Turn on the optional arrow and it gives you a clear reference for which way is forward, which is easy to lose track of once you start rotating the preview or working in 3D. Look for the option with the other grid and preview settings.

It helps most when placing and orienting models in a 3D layout. With a fixed sense of front, you can position props so they face the audience the way you intend.
