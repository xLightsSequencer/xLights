Download More Shaders

The Shader effect runs GPU shader programs to create flowing, organic visuals that would be hard to build any other way. You are not limited to the ones that ship with xLights.

Open the download dialog from the toolbar and you will find a large collection of shaders ready to grab. Once downloaded they appear in the Shader effect, where you can point the effect at the shader file and adjust its exposed controls.

Shaders are a quick way to add eye-catching backgrounds and textures to a matrix or tree, and the downloadable library keeps growing, so there is always something new to experiment with.
