FPP Connect Keyboard Shortcut

You can open FPP Connect with a keyboard shortcut instead of hunting for it in the menus.

Assign a key to it in the keyboard bindings preferences, where you set up your other shortcuts. Once it's bound, that key opens the FPP Connect dialog directly.

If you upload to your players often, a single keystroke to reach FPP Connect can shave a lot of clicks off your usual upload routine.
