Light Up Nodes While You Select Them

When you define faces, states, or submodels with the node selection dialog, you can have xLights send the nodes you pick out to your real lights as you choose them.

Enable the output to lights option in the selection dialog and the physical pixels light up live while you click. This means you can confirm you are grabbing the right part of a prop by looking at the display itself, not just the grid on screen.

It removes a lot of guesswork when a model's wiring does not match what you expected, and it saves repeated trips back and forth to fix a selection that turned out wrong.
