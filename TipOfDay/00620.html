More Transition Styles

Effects fade in and out using a transition, and there is a wide selection to choose from beyond the basic fade. Styles like Circles, Shatter and Swap each reveal or hide the effect underneath in a different way.

You set these on the In and Out transition dropdowns at the bottom of any effect's settings. Some transitions also have their own options, such as reversing the direction, so it is worth opening the dropdown and trying a few.

Mixing transitions across your sequence keeps things visually interesting, and the right one can give an effect a much more polished start and finish without any extra work.
