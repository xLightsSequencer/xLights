Ordered Node Selection For Submodels

When you build a submodel by picking individual nodes, you can have xLights remember the order in which you click them. The submodel node selection dialog offers an ordered selection mode for this.

The sequence you click matters because effects flow along the submodel in that order. With ordered selection you can route a chase or a wipe through nodes in any path you like, even one that does not follow the model's normal wiring.

It is ideal for tracing a custom shape, a letter, or a winding path through a prop so that motion effects travel exactly where you intend them to.
