SVG Images In The Shapes Effect

The Shapes effect can use SVG vector images as its shape, not just the built-in shapes.

Point the effect at an SVG file and it will draw that artwork as part of the effect. Because SVGs are vector based, they stay crisp at the size your model needs, which makes them a great source for logos, symbols, and custom outlines.

This opens up a lot of creative options, letting you bring your own shapes into a sequence without having to hand-build them from the standard set.
