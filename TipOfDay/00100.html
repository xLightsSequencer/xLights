Show Model Names And Start Channels In Layout

The Layout tab has "Show Names" and "Show Start Channel" checkboxes that label your models right in the preview. One draws each model's name, and the other draws its controller and port or its start channel.

You will find the checkboxes on the Layout tab. Turn them on to overlay the labels directly on top of the models in the preview, and turn them off again when you want a clean view.

It is a quick way to confirm where each model lives and how it is addressed without clicking into every one. Handy when you are wiring up a new layout or tracking down a channel conflict.
