Jump To A Prop In The Sequencer

The sequencer now has a prop filter that lets you quickly jump to a model or group by name.

Start typing part of a prop's name and the list narrows down to matching props, so you can land on the row you want without scrolling through a long element list.

On a big display with dozens of models this saves a lot of time. Instead of hunting up and down the timeline for the right row, you can go straight to it and start sequencing.
