Disable Rendering On An Element

You can turn off rendering for an individual element, so its effects are skipped when the sequence renders without you having to delete them.

Right-click the model's row in the sequencer to disable its rendering. The effects stay exactly where they are, ready to switch back on later.

This is great for temporarily silencing a prop you are not working on, speeding up render times while you focus elsewhere, or A/B testing how the show looks with a model in or out.
