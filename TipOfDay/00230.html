Split Multiple Effects At Once

You can split several effects in a single step. Select the effects you want, then right click and choose the option to split them.

Each selected effect is cut into two at its midpoint, giving you twice as many effects to work with. This saves you from splitting them one at a time when you want to add a transition partway through a run of effects, or break a long section into smaller pieces.

It is handy when you have copied or stretched effects across many rows and want to introduce some variation without rebuilding everything from scratch.
