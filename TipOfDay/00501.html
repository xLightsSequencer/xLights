The FX Effect

The FX option in the Single Strand effect brings a big library of pre-built animations to your strands, ported from the popular WS2812FX and FastLED patterns used by WLED.

Drop a Single Strand effect and switch to its FX tab, then pick a pattern from the list. Each one is a self-contained moving pattern designed for linear pixel runs, with a speed control to tune how fast it plays.

These are perfect for outlines, eaves, and other long single runs of pixels where you want an instant eye-catching motion without building it from scratch.
