Link Props Into Sets

You can now link several props together into a "set" so they move as one. When you drag, rotate or reposition any member of the set in the layout, the rest follow along keeping their relative arrangement.

This is handy for groups of props that always belong together — a row of mini trees, a window frame and its lights, or an arch and its base — where you want to nudge the whole cluster without re-aligning each piece by hand.

Link them once and you can lay out and reposition the whole arrangement in a single move.
