Zoom To Your Selection

On the Sequencer tab you can zoom the timeline so it fits the range you have selected, filling the view with just that part of your sequence.

Select a span of time or a group of effects, then use the zoom to selection command. The timeline scales so your selection neatly fills the grid.

It is a quick way to dive into a busy section for detailed editing and then zoom back out, without dragging the zoom slider back and forth to find the right level.
