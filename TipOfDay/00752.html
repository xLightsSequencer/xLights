A Second Outline For Faces

Face definitions can include a second outline in addition to the main one, giving you another ring of nodes to light around the face.

You set the extra outline up in the face definition for your model, and the Face effect can drive it with its own color. That second outline uses a separate palette color from the first.

It's useful for singing faces and props that have an inner and an outer border, letting you light the two rings in different colors so the face reads clearly from a distance.
