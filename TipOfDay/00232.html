Breakdown Selected Phrases

When working with lyric timing tracks, you can break down just the phrases you have selected rather than the whole track at once.

Select the phrase timing marks you want, right click, and choose to break them down into words and phonemes. The rest of the track is left untouched.

This gives you finer control when you only need to redo part of a song, or when you want to tweak the breakdown of a tricky section without disturbing timings you have already cleaned up by hand.
