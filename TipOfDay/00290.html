A Friendlier FPP Connect Dialog

The FPP Connect dialog has a number of improvements that make uploading to your players easier.

IP addresses and hostnames now appear in separate columns with clickable links, there's a re-discover button to run discovery again without leaving the dialog, and you can choose to keep the dialog open after an upload finishes. There are also more ways to select which sequences to send, including picking the ones from your last batch render.

Together these make pushing sequences out to multiple FPP devices quicker and less fiddly, especially when you upload to the same set of players regularly.
