Measure Your Layout With A Ruler

You can tell xLights the real-world size of your display by defining a ruler on the layout tab. Once a ruler is set, distances are shown in actual units rather than abstract preview pixels.

In 3D mode the grid spacing is then drawn in those real units, and model dimensions read out in feet, metres or whatever you chose. This makes it far easier to lay props out at true scale.

Getting the sizes right pays off when you space models apart, plan where to mount things, or check that a prop will actually fit. The preview starts to reflect what you will really see in the yard.
