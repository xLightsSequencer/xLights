Paste Multi-Layer Effects Anywhere

When you paste effects that span several layers, xLights now adds the layers you need automatically.

If you copy a stack of layered effects and paste it onto a model that does not have enough layers, the extra layers are created for you so nothing is lost or flattened. There is also a "Copy Layers/SubModels to Models" option that pastes effects onto several models at once.

Together these make it much easier to reuse a layered look across your display, since you can build it once and drop it onto every model without setting up the layers each time.
