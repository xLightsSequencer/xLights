Color Your Text By Word

The Text effect has a "Color by word" option that gives each word in your message its own colour from the palette, instead of colouring everything the same way.

You will find it among the Text effect's colour options. As the words scroll or appear, each one picks up the next colour in your palette, so a line of text becomes a row of alternating colours.

It is a fast way to make a message more lively without typing out separate effects. Pick a palette you like and the words take care of cycling through it for you.
