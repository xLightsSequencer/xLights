Render A Selected Range

When you export a model, you do not have to render the whole sequence. Select a range of effects first and you can export just that slice of the timeline.

This is a big time saver on long sequences. Instead of waiting for the entire song to render and export when you only want to check one chorus, you limit the work to the section you have highlighted.

It pairs well with the GIF, video and image export options, letting you produce a short clip of exactly the part you are working on for review or sharing.
