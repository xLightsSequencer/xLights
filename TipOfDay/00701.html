Disable Rendering Of Individual Effects

You can switch off a single effect so it stays on the grid but does not render or play back. Right-click the effect and choose the option to stop it rendering.

The effect remains exactly where it is, with all its settings intact, but it is skipped when xLights renders and outputs. Turn it back on the same way whenever you want it to count again.

This is great for trying things out. You can park an alternative effect next to the one you are using, mute it, and flip between them without deleting or recreating anything.
