The Adjust Effect

The Adjust effect modifies the result of whatever is rendering underneath it without you having to change those effects directly.

Drop it on a layer above your existing effects and it tweaks the values coming through. This is especially useful on DMX models, where you can nudge or reshape channel output across a section of the timeline while leaving the original effects untouched.

Because it works on the rendered result, you can experiment freely. If the adjustment is not what you wanted, delete the layer and the effects below are exactly as they were.
