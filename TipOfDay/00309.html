Link Servo Effects Together

Servo effects can be linked so that the end of one effect drives the start of the next, and the other way around. When effects are linked, adjusting the end position of one automatically updates the beginning of the one that follows.

This keeps a chain of servo movements continuous, with no sudden jump between where one effect leaves the servo and where the next picks it up.

It is a real help when choreographing smooth, multi-step motion on servo-driven props like moving heads or animated figures.
