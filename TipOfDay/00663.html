Flip And Reverse Submodel Nodes

The Submodel dialog can flip, shift, and reverse the nodes of a submodel, so you can fix the direction or starting point of a selection without re-picking every node.

Open a model's submodels and select the submodel you want to adjust. The flip, shift, and reverse options change how the nodes are ordered, and similar shift and reverse options are also available when editing Faces and States.

This saves a lot of clicking when a submodel runs the wrong way or starts at the wrong end. Reversing or shifting the node order lets effects play in the direction you intended.
