Alternate Audio Tracks

A sequence can now carry more than one audio track, so you can keep an alternate version of the song alongside the main one.

You manage the tracks in Sequence Settings. Each alternate track gets its own waveform, VU Meter and value curve access, just like the primary audio, so you can drive effects from whichever track you choose.

This is handy when you have a vocals-only mix, an instrumental, or a louder reference track that picks out beats more clearly. You can sequence against the version that works best and still keep the original audio with the show.
