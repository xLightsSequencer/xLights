Ripple Cycles And Scale

The Ripple effect has settings for cycles and scale that change how its expanding rings behave across a model.

Cycles controls how many times the ripple repeats over the duration of the effect, so you can pack several pulses into one block. Scale changes how large each ring grows, letting you fit the ripple to small props or stretch it across a big matrix.

Together they give you a lot of range from one effect. Try a high cycle count for a fast heartbeat look, or a large scale for a slow wave that washes over the whole display.
