Crop Pictures To Fill The Model

The Picture effect has a scaling option that keeps the image's aspect ratio while cropping it to fill the whole model.

Add a Picture effect, choose your image, and set the scaling to the keep-aspect-ratio crop option. The picture is enlarged until it covers the entire model and the parts that overflow the edges are trimmed off, instead of being squashed or leaving blank borders.

This is the look you usually want for a photo on a matrix or megatree. The image fills the display and stays in proportion, so faces and shapes are not distorted.
