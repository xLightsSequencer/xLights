Replace Multiple Models At Once

The Replace Model feature lets you swap one model out for another, and the dialog can now handle several models in a single pass.

When you open it you get a filtered, checkbox list of the models you want to replace. Type to narrow the list down, tick the targets you care about, and confirm. Everything pointing at the old models gets updated together instead of one at a time.

This is a big time saver when you upgrade props or restructure a display, since you no longer have to repeat the same replacement over and over for each model in your layout.
