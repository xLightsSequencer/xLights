Newer Autosave Warning

When you open a sequence, xLights checks whether an autosave file exists that is newer than the file you are opening, and it now shows you the dates of both.

If your last session ended unexpectedly, the autosave may hold work that never made it into the saved sequence. Seeing the timestamps side by side makes it clear which version is more recent, so you can decide whether to recover the autosave or stick with the file you saved.

This takes the guesswork out of crash recovery. Instead of wondering if the autosave is worth loading, you can look at the dates and pick the one that actually has your latest changes.
