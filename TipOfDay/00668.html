Export Controller Connections To A Spreadsheet

xLights can export a CSV file listing your Ethernet controllers and the models attached to each port.

Look in the Tools menu for the option to export controller connections. The resulting CSV opens in any spreadsheet program and shows which model lives on which controller and port.

This is great for documenting your show or for working through wiring out in the yard away from the computer. Having the port assignments on paper makes troubleshooting and rebuilding much easier.
