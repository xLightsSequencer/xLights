Import An LOR S5 Preview

xLights can read a Light-O-Rama S5 preview file and bring its props into your layout, so you do not have to rebuild a display you already laid out in LOR.

Look for the option to import an S5 .lorprev file when adding models to your layout. xLights reads the props from the file and creates matching models for you.

It is a real time-saver when you are moving a show over from LOR, or collaborating with someone who built their layout there.
