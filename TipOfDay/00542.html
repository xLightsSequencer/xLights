Spot The First Pixel Of Each Model

On the Layout tab, xLights can show the first pixel of every model in a bright cyan dot. This marks where each model starts counting its nodes.

Knowing the start point matters because effects flow out from the first pixel, and your wiring needs to match. Seeing the cyan markers across your whole display makes it obvious at a glance whether a model is oriented and starting where you expect.

It is especially useful after importing or rotating a model, when the start point can end up somewhere you did not intend. A quick look at the markers catches the problem before it shows up in a sequence.
