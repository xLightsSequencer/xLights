DMX Colour Wheel Support

DMX models can be told about a physical colour wheel, so xLights maps the colours you sequence to the matching wheel positions on the fixture.

You set this up in the model's properties, describing which DMX channel drives the wheel and what colours sit in each slot. Once defined, ordinary colour choices in your effects translate into the correct wheel value automatically.

This means you can sequence a moving head or scanner using familiar colours instead of memorising raw channel numbers, and the preview reflects what the fixture will actually show.
