Adjust The Auto Eye Blink

The Faces effect can blink the eyes automatically, and you can control how often that blink happens.

When you set up a singing face with eye images, look for the automatic blink frequency setting. Turn it up for a more active, nervous look or down for a calmer character that only blinks now and then.

A little tuning here makes a face feel more natural. The default works, but matching the blink rate to the mood of the song adds a surprising amount of life to your singing props.
