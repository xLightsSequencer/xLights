FPP Command And Effect Timing Tracks

You can add special timing tracks for FPP command presets and FPP effects, and xLights saves them inside the FSEQ file so FPP acts on them during playback.

Create the timing track in your sequence and place marks where you want things to happen. When the sequence plays back on FPP, each mark can fire a command preset or trigger an FPP effect at that exact moment.

This lets your light show drive other parts of your setup in perfect time, such as starting a pixel overlay or running a preset, all timed straight from the sequence instead of scheduled separately.
