The Guitar Effect

The Guitar effect draws animated strings that pluck and vibrate, giving you a guitar or other stringed-instrument look on your display.

Drop it onto a model from the effects panel and you can shape the strings, control how they wave when struck, and even enter notes and chords so the right strings respond. Prefix a note with the letter C to play it as a chord, so CA strums an A chord.

It is a fun choice for music sequences where you want a visual that follows the melody, and the string and wave controls let you tune the look to match your song.
