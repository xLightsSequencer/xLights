Match A Video Effect To Its Video

The Video effect can stretch itself to the exact length of the video file you point it at, so the effect and the clip end at the same moment.

Pick your video, then use the option to match the effect duration to the video. xLights resizes the effect on the grid to fit, which saves you from measuring the clip and dragging the edges by hand.

It is the easiest way to play a clip from start to finish without cutting it short or leaving a blank tail, and it keeps your timeline tidy when you swap one video for another.
