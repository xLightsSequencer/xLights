Virtual Matrix And LED Panel Models

As well as pixel strings, xLights can drive matrices that are connected as a video output or an LED panel rather than as individual pixels. When you create or edit a model you can pick the "Virtual Matrix" or "LED Panel Matrix" style for these kinds of displays.

These work with controllers that support them, such as Raspberry Pi based FPP devices that can send a matrix out over an HDMI port. The matrix shows up in the controller visualizer alongside your pixel ports so you can see how everything is wired together.

This lets you sequence a screen or panel display the same way you sequence the rest of your show, with all your usual effects, instead of treating it as a separate device.
