Embed Images In Your Sequence

Images used by the Pictures effect can now be stored right inside the xsq file instead of only being referenced from disk.

A new "Images" tab in Sequence Settings lets you see and manage every image the sequence uses. The Picture effect panel shows the selected image, images are loaded once when the sequence opens and cached for rendering, and SUP imports automatically embed the hundreds of images they create.

Embedding means you can hand a single sequence file to someone else and the pictures travel with it, so nothing shows up missing on the other end.
