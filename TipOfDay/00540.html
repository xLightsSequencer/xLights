Flip Models On The Layout

The Layout tab can mirror a model for you with Flip Horizontal and Flip Vertical commands. Select a model in the preview and you will find the flip options on the right-click menu.

Flipping turns a model into its mirror image without you having to move or re-wire anything. It is handy when you have two matching props on opposite sides of your display and want one to be a true reflection of the other.

It also saves time when an imported or custom model ends up facing the wrong way. Instead of rebuilding it, a single flip puts it the right way round.
