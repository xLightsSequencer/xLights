Check Sequences From An FPP Playlist

You can batch render and check the sequences that make up a playlist on one of your FPP devices.

Right click on an FPP device's playlist and choose the option to batch render and run Check Sequence against those sequences. xLights gathers the sequences from that playlist for you instead of you opening and checking each one by hand.

This is a quick way to confirm that everything in a show playlist is healthy before the night you actually need it to run.
