AI Assisted Sequence Mapping

When you import effects from someone else's sequence, xLights can use AI to suggest how their models should map onto yours.

You'll find it in the import mapping dialog, where models from the source sequence are matched up to the models in your layout. Instead of working down a long list by hand, you can let the AI propose matches based on the model names and types, then review and adjust anything it got wrong.

It's especially handy with large layouts or shared sequences where the naming doesn't line up exactly. You stay in control of the final mapping, but the tedious first pass is done for you.
