Print The Controller Visualizer

The controller visualizer can be printed, and a large layout spreads across multiple pages so nothing is cut off.

Open the visualizer for a controller and use its print option. A wide board with many ports and models prints over several sheets instead of being squeezed onto one. The visualizer also remembers its window size and position between openings.

A printed copy is handy in the shed or yard when you are wiring up, away from the computer. You can follow the port-by-port plan on paper as you connect each model.
