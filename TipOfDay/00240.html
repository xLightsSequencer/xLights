Redo Your Last Undo

If you undo a change and then decide you wanted it after all, you can now redo it. Look for Redo on the Edit menu, or use its keyboard shortcut.

Redo steps forward through the actions you have just undone, putting them back exactly as they were. It pairs naturally with Undo, so you can move back and forth while you compare two versions of an edit.

This takes the worry out of experimenting. You can undo a few steps to see how something looked before, then redo to return to where you were.
