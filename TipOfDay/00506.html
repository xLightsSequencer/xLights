Tag Colours For Model Groups

You can assign a tag colour to a model group to help you tell your groups apart at a glance.

Set the colour in the group's properties on the Layout tab. The colour then helps the group stand out in the lists where your groups appear, which is handy once a show grows to dozens of them.

Pick a scheme that means something to you, such as one colour for roofline groups and another for trees, and navigating a busy layout gets much easier.
