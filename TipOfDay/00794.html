Open Pixel Control Output

xLights can send pixel data using Open Pixel Control, often shortened to OPC. It is a simple network protocol used by a number of hobby and art lighting projects to receive colour data over a connection.

You add it like any other output on the controllers tab by choosing OPC as the protocol and giving the destination address. From there your models map to it the same way they do for other network protocols.

This opens up driving devices and software that speak OPC rather than the usual show controllers, which is handy for custom installations and integrating xLights with other creative tools.
