Zoom The Timeline To Your Mouse

The sequencer can zoom the timeline toward wherever your mouse is pointing instead of toward the center.

With this turned on, point at the spot you care about and zoom in, and that spot stays under the cursor as the timeline expands. Look for the option in the sequencer preferences to enable it.

It keeps you oriented when working closely on one part of a song. You zoom right into the section you are editing without it sliding off to the side and forcing you to scroll back.
