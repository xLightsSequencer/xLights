Twinkle That Covers Every Pixel

The Twinkle effect has a more random algorithm that spreads its twinkles across all the pixels in a model rather than favouring the same spots.

Add a Twinkle effect and look in its settings for the style or algorithm option. The newer behaviour gives a more even, scattered sparkle, and the original algorithm is still available if you prefer the older look.

It is worth trying on trees, matrices, and outlines where you want every light to get a turn. The fuller coverage makes the twinkle feel livelier without you needing to change anything else.
