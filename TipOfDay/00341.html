Two Mouth Colors For Faces

When you use Force Custom Colors on the Faces effect, you can set separate colours for the mouth and the rest of the face. The Faces panel lets you choose both so the lips stand out from the eyes and outline.

This is handy for singing faces where you want the mouth to read clearly as it moves through the phonemes. A different mouth colour keeps it visible even when the face around it is lit in another shade.

Combine it with your timing track lyrics and the mouth shapes pop against the face, making the singing much easier to follow on a pixel display.
