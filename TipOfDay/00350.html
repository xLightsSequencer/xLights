Add A Palette To The Picture Assistant

The Picture Assistant, where you draw a small image to use with the Pictures effect, includes a colour palette. Instead of reaching for the full colour picker every time, you can keep your working colours on hand as you paint.

This makes drawing icons and simple graphics much quicker, since the shades you keep coming back to are one click away. It also helps you stay consistent across a picture.

If you build little images to scroll across a matrix, the palette turns the assistant into a more comfortable place to sketch them out without breaking your flow.
