Import From Light-O-Rama S5

If you are coming from Light-O-Rama, xLights can read an S5 preview to bring your models and layout across. It pulls in the props you have already laid out so you do not have to rebuild them by hand.

Look for the Light-O-Rama S5 import option among the import choices. Once your models are in, you can map your old sequences onto them using the normal effect import mapping workflow.

This gives you a head start when switching over, turning a layout you already built in another program into a starting point for your xLights show.
