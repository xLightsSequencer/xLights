RGBWW Pixel Support

xLights has support for RGBWW pixels, the type that add warm and cool white channels alongside the usual red, green, and blue. When you set a model's pixel type to an RGBWW variant, xLights accounts for those extra white channels.

You choose the colour order and pixel type in the model's string properties, the same place you set up plain RGB strings. Picking the right RGBWW option keeps the channel counts and ordering correct for your controller.

Getting the type right matters most when you upload to a controller, since the white channels need to land on the correct outputs for your whites to look the way you intend.
