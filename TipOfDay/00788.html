The DMX Area Flood Model

Alongside the round flood, xLights has a DMX Area Flood model that represents a rectangular light rather than a circular one. You can add it from the model types when laying out your show.

The rectangular shape better matches panel and bar style fixtures, and it shows in the preview as a rectangle so your layout looks closer to the real thing. Like other DMX models it drives the fixture's colour and dimmer channels.

Choosing the model shape that matches your hardware makes the preview easier to read and helps you place and aim your floods accurately in the layout.
