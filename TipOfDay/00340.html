Highlight Color For The Bars Effect

The Bars effect can highlight one edge of each bar in a colour of your choosing. Turn on the highlight option in the Bars settings and pick the colour you want for that leading edge.

This gives each moving bar a bright running line at its front, separate from the colours that fill the rest of the bar. It is a quick way to add definition and a sense of direction to bars sweeping across a model.

Try it with a contrasting highlight against a darker fill. The extra edge colour reads well from a distance and makes the motion of the bars much easier to see.
