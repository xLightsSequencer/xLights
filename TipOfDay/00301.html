Reverse Submodels When You Reverse A Model

When you reverse the node order of a model, you can optionally have its submodels reversed to match. The choice appears when you reverse the model so you don't have to fix each submodel by hand.

Without this, reversing a model would leave its submodels pointing the old way, so a submodel that used to cover the left side would suddenly be on the right. Letting xLights reverse them at the same time keeps everything consistent.

It saves a lot of fiddly rework after you correct the wiring direction on a prop that has several submodels defined on it.
