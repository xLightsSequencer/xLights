SVG Shapes In The VU Meter

The VU Meter effect can use SVG files as the shapes it draws for its level-shape style, instead of being limited to the built-in shapes.

Choose a level-shape render style on the VU Meter effect and point it at an SVG file. The meter then grows and shrinks your custom artwork in time with the music.

Because SVG is vector artwork it stays crisp at any size, so you can react to the beat with your own logos, icons, or themed shapes rather than plain bars and circles.
