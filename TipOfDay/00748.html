3D Floodlight Beams

Floodlights can show a real beam in the 3D preview, with control over how far the beam reaches.

On a floodlight model you can set the beam length so the light appears to project out into the scene rather than just lighting a flat spot. In a 3D layout this gives the flood a visible cone you can aim and judge.

It makes previews of floods, wash lights, and up-lighting far more realistic, helping you see roughly where the light will land before you ever power up the real fixture.
