Import From A Packaged Sequence Zip

When someone shares a packaged sequence, you get a single zip file containing the sequence and everything it needs. You can import effects straight from that zip without unpacking it first.

Start an effect import as usual and choose the packaged zip as the source. xLights reads inside the archive and lets you map the other person's models onto your own.

This makes borrowing effects from a shared sequence much simpler, since you do not have to extract files, sort out media, or hunt for the right xsq before you can begin mapping.
