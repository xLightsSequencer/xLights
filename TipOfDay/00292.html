Control The Group Crosshair

A preference lets you hide or resize the crosshair shown for groups on the Layout tab.

When a group is selected, xLights draws a crosshair marking its center in the preview. In preferences you can now make it smaller or turn it off entirely if it gets in the way of seeing your models.

This is handy on dense layouts where the crosshair overlaps your props, letting you keep the preview uncluttered while still working with groups.
