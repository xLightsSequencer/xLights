Zoom To Your Cursor On The Layout

There is a preference that controls whether the Layout view zooms toward the centre of the screen or toward your mouse cursor. Choose the behaviour that feels natural to you in Preferences.

Zooming to the cursor lets you point at a model, scroll in, and have that model stay under your pointer as it grows. It is the same feel many map and drawing programs use.

If you work with large layouts and frequently dive into one corner to tweak a single prop, the zoom-to-cursor setting can save a lot of panning back and forth.
