Remove Models From Groups Quickly

You can now remove a model from a group straight from a right-click popup menu.

Right-click a model and you'll find an option to take it out of an existing group, so you no longer have to open the group editor just to drop a model you no longer want in it. You can remove one or more models this way.

It makes tidying up your groups much faster, especially when you're reorganizing your layout and shuffling models between groups.
