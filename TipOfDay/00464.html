Scale And Offset DMX Values

The DMX remap dialog lets you scale and offset the values being sent to a channel, not just pass them straight through.

When you remap channels on a DMX model, you can multiply the incoming value and add an offset so the output lands in the range your fixture actually expects. This is useful when a moving head, dimmer, or other device wants a narrower band of values than xLights would otherwise produce.

Getting the range right in the remap means your fixtures behave correctly without you having to work around their limits in every effect you sequence.
