Sort A Model Group By Location

When you edit a model group, you can sort its members by their location in the layout. xLights orders the models based on where they physically sit rather than the order you happened to add them.

This matters because effects applied to a whole group travel through the models in group order. Sorting by location makes a chase or wipe move smoothly across the group in space.

It saves you from dragging models up and down the group list by hand to get a sensible flow. Let xLights arrange them by position and your group-wide effects look far more natural.
