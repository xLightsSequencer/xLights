Pan The Sequencer With The Middle Mouse

You can pan around the sequencer grid by holding the middle mouse button and dragging. The view follows your drag so you can move along the timeline and across rows without reaching for the scroll bars.

This is quick when you are zoomed in on a busy part of the sequence and want to slide over to a nearby section.

It keeps your hand on the mouse and your eyes on the grid, which makes navigating a long, detailed sequence feel a lot more fluid.
