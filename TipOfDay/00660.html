Single Strand Chase And Bounce Styles

The Single Strand effect offers several chase styles that move light back and forth along a strand, including a dual chase that runs two chases at once and bounce styles that travel to and from the middle of the model.

Drop a Single Strand effect on a model and look at the Chase style dropdown. Try the dual chase for two passes running together, or pick a bounce-to-middle or bounce-from-middle style to have the light converge on or spread out from the centre.

These styles are an easy way to add motion to arches, canes, and long pixel runs without building anything by hand. Switching the style completely changes the look while keeping all your other settings the same.
