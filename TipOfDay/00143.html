Hold Shift To Keep Aspect Ratio

When you resize a model on the Layout tab, hold down the Shift key to keep its aspect ratio locked.

Just start dragging a corner handle as usual and press Shift while you drag. The model grows and shrinks proportionally so it doesn't get stretched taller or wider than it should be.

This is the easiest way to scale a model up or down while keeping its shape exactly right, which matters for things like stars, snowflakes, and any model where the proportions need to stay true.
