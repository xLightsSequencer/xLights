The Label Text Model

There is a new Label text model you can add to your layout. It places a piece of text in the preview as its own model.

Add it the same way you add any other model from the Layout tab, then set the text you want it to display. It sits in your layout alongside your real props.

Labels are useful for annotating areas of a large display, marking sections while you build, or adding readable text to a layout without having to build it out of pixels. It keeps the preview organized as your show grows.
