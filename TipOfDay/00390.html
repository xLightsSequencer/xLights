Friendly Device Names When Importing

The Import From Controller dialog shows friendly names for devices it recognizes instead of just raw addresses.

When you pull a configuration in from a controller, known device types are labeled with their recognizable names. That makes the list far easier to read than a column of numbers and identifiers.

If you manage several controllers, this helps you pick the right one with confidence. You can see at a glance what each entry actually is before you import from it.
