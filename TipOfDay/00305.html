Add Silence Before Or After Your Song

You can add a stretch of silence to the start or end of your media without editing the audio file in another program. Look for the pre and post silence options when working with your sequence's audio.

Adding silence at the front gives you room for an intro animation before the music kicks in, and silence at the end leaves space for a fade-out or a closing pose.

It is a simple way to build breathing room into a song so your show doesn't start or stop abruptly right on the first and last beats.
