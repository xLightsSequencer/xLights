Change Audio Playback Speed

You can slow down or speed up audio playback while you sequence, and there are keybindings to do it without reaching for the menus. Slowing the song down makes it much easier to place effects on fast or busy passages.

Assign or check the playback speed keybindings in the keyboard shortcut preferences, then use them as the audio plays. The pitch may shift, but the timing stays true so you can line effects up precisely.

Once a tricky section is timed, you can return to full speed to enjoy the result. It is a simple trick that takes a lot of the frustration out of detailed sequencing.
