Ring The Bell On Render Errors

You can have xLights ring a bell if something goes wrong during a batch render.

Turn on the option in the batch render settings, then start your render and walk away. If an error comes up while you are not watching, the sound lets you know instead of leaving you to discover it later when the batch has finished.

This is useful when you queue up a large batch of sequences to render and don't want to sit and stare at the progress the whole time.
