Chroma Key Transparency

The Pictures and Video effects can make a chosen colour transparent, just like a green screen in film. Look for the chroma key option in the effect settings and pick the colour you want to drop out.

Any pixels close to that colour become see-through, so whatever effect sits on the layer below shows through. A tolerance control lets you decide how close a colour has to be before it is treated as transparent.

This is a simple way to layer a subject over a moving background, or to clean up an image or video that has a solid backdrop you would rather not display on your props.
