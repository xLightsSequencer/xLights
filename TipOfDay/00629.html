Value Curves On The Twinkle Effect

The Twinkle effect supports value curves on its settings, so the look can change over the life of a single effect instead of staying fixed.

Click the small value curve button next to a Twinkle slider and define how it should move. You might ramp the number of lights or their speed up over time, or have the twinkle build and then settle down.

Animating these settings gives a much more dynamic sparkle than a static Twinkle, and saves you from chopping the timeline into lots of separate effects to get the change you want.
