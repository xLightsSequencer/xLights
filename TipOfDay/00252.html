Export Faces And States To Other Models

If you have set up face or state definitions on one model, you can copy them over to other models instead of building them again from scratch.

Open the Faces or States dialog for the model that already has them defined, and use the export option to push those definitions out to other models you choose.

This is a real time saver when you have several similar props, such as a row of singing faces, that all need the same mouth and eye setups.
