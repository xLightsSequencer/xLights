Play GIFs In The Movie Effect

The Movie effect can play animated GIF files, not just video files. When you browse for a file you can select a GIF and it will animate on your model.

This is handy because there are huge numbers of short looping animations available as GIFs, and they often suit a matrix or megatree perfectly. You skip the step of converting them to a video format first.

As with any source, the result looks best on models with enough nodes to show detail, so GIFs are a great match for matrices and other dense props.
