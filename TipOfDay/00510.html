Low Definition Rendering Mode

Matrices can be set to render in a low definition mode, which renders effects at a reduced resolution while you work.

On a big high-resolution matrix this makes editing far more responsive, because each render does far less work. You still see what the effect is doing without waiting on a full-resolution pass after every change.

Turn it off again before your final render so the show goes out at full quality. It is purely an editing aid and does not change the effects you have placed.
