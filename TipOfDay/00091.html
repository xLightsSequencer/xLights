Choose Groups When Importing From Layout

When you import models from another layout, you can now pick which groups those models get added to instead of taking everything as-is.

The option appears during the import-from-layout flow, letting you select the destination groups for the incoming models. That keeps your group structure tidy and puts the new models exactly where you want them.

It is handy when you are pulling props from a prior show or a shared layout and only want them in certain groups, saving you from sorting models into groups by hand afterward.
