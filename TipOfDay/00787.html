Select Effects By Type

The Select Effects tool lets you find and highlight effects across your sequence by their type. Open it and choose, for example, every On effect or every Bars effect.

All matching effects get selected at once, so you can then bulk edit them, delete them, or jump between them. This is far quicker than hunting through every model and timing track by eye.

It is especially useful late in a sequence when you decide a particular effect needs a tweak everywhere it appears. Select them all by type, make one change, and they update together.
