More AI Services For Palettes And Mapping

xLights can now use Claude as an AI service for generating color palettes and for mapping models during import, and there is also a generic OpenAI-style client for connecting to local or remote services that speak the OpenAI v1 API.

You set up which service to use in the AI settings, then the AI features such as palette generation and model mapping will route through your chosen provider. The generic client means you can even point xLights at an AI model running on your own machine.

Having more than one option lets you pick the service you already have access to, and a local endpoint keeps your requests off the internet entirely.
