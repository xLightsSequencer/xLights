Shadow Models

A shadow model lets one model copy the output of another, so the two play exactly the same data. You point a model at the model it should shadow in its properties.

This is useful when you have physical props that should always do the same thing, such as matching trees or a mirrored pair, without sequencing each one separately. You sequence the original and the shadow follows along automatically.

In the controller visualiser, shadow models and the models they shadow are shown so you can tell them apart. That makes it clear which props are driven directly and which are mirroring another model.
