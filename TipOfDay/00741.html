Add Models To An Existing Group

You don't have to rebuild a group from scratch when you want to add a few more models to it. Select the models you want on the Layout tab, right-click, and choose to add them to an existing group.

Pick the destination group from the list and the selected models are appended to it without disturbing the models already there or their order.

This makes it quick to grow a group as your display expands, instead of opening the group editor and hunting through the full model list each time.
