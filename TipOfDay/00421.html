Parallel FSEQ Uploads

When you upload your sequences through FPP Connect, xLights can send the FSEQ files to several controllers at the same time instead of one after another.

You don't need to do anything special to use this. Just pick the controllers you want in the FPP Connect dialog and start the upload as usual, and the files go out in parallel.

On a show with many FPP instances this can dramatically shorten the time you spend waiting for uploads to finish, which adds up when you are testing changes and re-uploading often.
