Stop Your Arches From Zig-Zagging

Arch models have an option that controls whether the strands zig-zag back and forth or all run in the same direction. If your wiring goes straight through rather than reversing on each arch, you can tell xLights not to zig-zag.

You will find the setting in the arch model's properties. Matching it to how your arches are actually wired makes effects flow across them the way you expect.

Getting this right means a chase or a sweep travels cleanly along the whole set of arches instead of looking jumbled where the direction would otherwise flip.
