Shift-Drag Effect Edges For Fades

Want a quick fade in or fade out without opening the effect settings panel? Hold Shift and drag the left or right edge of an effect in the sequencer grid.

Dragging the left edge with Shift held adjusts the fade-in time, and dragging the right edge adjusts the fade-out time, right there on the grid.

It's much faster than selecting the effect, finding the fade controls in the panel, and typing in numbers, especially when you are tweaking a fade by eye against the music.
