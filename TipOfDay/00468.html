Rename Your Previews

The extra previews you create on the Layout tab can be renamed, so you are not stuck with the name you first gave them.

Rename a preview when its purpose changes, or to give it a clear label like a yard zone or a particular display area. A good name makes the preview easy to find when you are switching between layouts or assigning models.

As your show grows and you add more previews, sensible names keep everything organised and save you from hunting through a list of look-alike entries.
