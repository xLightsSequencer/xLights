Embedded Sequence Details On FPP

When you use FPP Connect to upload an fseq file to FPP 9.3 or newer, xLights now embeds extra sequence details inside the uploaded file.

You do not have to do anything different. Run FPP Connect as usual, and the richer information rides along with the fseq when it is sent to the player.

With those details available on the FPP side, the player can show more about each sequence, which makes it easier to identify and manage your shows directly from FPP.
