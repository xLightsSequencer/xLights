Drag To Select Nodes In Model Previews

The node previews in the Submodel, Faces, and States dialogs let you click and drag a box to select a group of nodes, and double-click to pick a single node.

When you are defining a submodel, a face, or a state, work directly on the preview picture of the model. Drag across the nodes you want and they are added to your selection, instead of typing node numbers or clicking them one at a time.

This makes building faces, states, and submodels much faster, especially on dense models. You can see exactly which lights you are choosing as you go.
