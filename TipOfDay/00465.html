Upper And Lower Case Text Tokens

The Text effect understands special tokens that force your text to upper or lower case. Insert ${UPPER} or ${LOWER} in front of the text you want changed.

This is especially handy when your text comes from a file, a timing track, or another source you do not control directly. Instead of editing the source, you let the effect convert the case for you as it renders.

It keeps your on-screen wording consistent, so song titles or countdown words always show the way you want them no matter how they were typed originally.
