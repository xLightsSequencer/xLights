Color Your Sparkles

The sparkles you can add to many effects do not have to be white. There is a sparkle colour setting that lets you choose what colour the little twinkles appear in.

You will find sparkles in the effect's appearance settings, where you can control how many there are and now also what colour they take. Match them to your palette or set a contrasting colour to make them pop.

Coloured sparkles are a quick way to add texture and movement on top of a solid or gentle effect, giving an otherwise plain look a bit of shimmer that fits the mood of your song.
