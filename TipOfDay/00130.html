Animated Effect Panel Thumbnails

The Pictures, Shader, and Video effect panels now show a live thumbnail preview of the file you have selected, and the same previews appear in the media manager.

Animated GIFs and WebP files cycle through all their frames right in the panel, videos play their first second, and SVGs show a static preview. You don't have to do anything special to turn this on, it shows up as soon as you pick a file.

This makes it much easier to confirm you grabbed the right file before you render, especially when you have a folder full of similarly named images or clips.
