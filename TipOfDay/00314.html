Keep The Duration When Duplicating An Effect

When you duplicate an effect, you can have the copy keep the same length as the original. This is an option you can turn on so duplicated effects come out at a consistent duration.

It is useful when you are laying down a series of identical effects, such as repeated pulses or beats, and you want each one to be exactly as long as the last.

With it on you can duplicate down a row and get evenly sized effects without having to drag every edge back to the same width.
