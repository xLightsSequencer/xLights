Pinwheel Rotation Offset

The Pinwheel effect has a rotation offset setting that rotates the whole pattern to a starting angle of your choosing.

You'll find it among the Pinwheel settings. Adjusting it spins the arms around so they begin pointing where you want, rather than always starting from the default orientation.

This is handy when you run Pinwheel across several props and want their arms to line up, or when you simply want the spokes aimed at a particular point on a model.
