Type Exact Value Curve Points

When you build a custom value curve, you do not have to drag every point by eye. Double-click a point and you can set its Y value precisely.

This lets you place a point at an exact level instead of nudging it until it looks close. It is ideal when you need a curve to hit a specific brightness or position at a certain spot, or when two points should match each other exactly.

Precise points make your fades and movements repeatable and clean, which matters most on slow ramps where a slightly off value is easy to see.
