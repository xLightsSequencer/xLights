Search For Lyrics Online

You can pull synced lyrics straight off the internet instead of typing them in by hand. Right-click a timing track and choose "Search for Lyrics Online..." and xLights looks up the song by title and artist.

The title and artist are filled in automatically from your sequence header when they are set, so often you just confirm the search and pick the matching result. The lyrics come in already synced, dropped onto the timing track as one phrase per line.

This is a big head start for lyric and lip-sync work. You get timed phrases to build from in seconds, and you can fine-tune the marks from there rather than starting from a blank track.
