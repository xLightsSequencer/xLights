Liquid Effect Gravity Direction

The Liquid effect lets you choose which way gravity pulls, so the simulated fluid does not always have to fall straight down.

Change the gravity direction on the Liquid settings and the particles drift toward the side you pick. You can make liquid pour sideways, rise upward, or settle toward a corner, which is great for matching the shape and orientation of an unusual prop.

It turns one effect into many different looks, since the same liquid behaves very differently depending on the direction you send its gravity.
