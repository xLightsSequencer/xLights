Measure Polyline Segments

The Polyline model lets you give measurements to its individual segments. Instead of guessing, you can tell xLights the real length of each run between corners.

This matters when a polyline follows an uneven path, such as around a roofline or a garden bed, where the segments are not all the same length. Entering the measurements spaces the nodes correctly so the on-screen model matches reality.

Accurate segments make effects look right too, because xLights then knows how the lights are really distributed along the path rather than assuming even spacing.
