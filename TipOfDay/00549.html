Resize The Inner Layer Of A Star

Multi-layer Star models have an Inner Layer percentage that controls how big the innermost layer is. You set it in the model's properties on the Layout tab.

Adjusting this lets you make the centre of the star tighter or more open, so the layers match the spacing of your real prop. It is the difference between a star that looks cramped in the middle and one whose rings sit evenly.

Because the value is a percentage, you can fine-tune it until the on-screen model lines up with how your physical star is actually built.
