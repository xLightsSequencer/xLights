Center Pixel On Circle Models

Circle models can have a layer with a size of zero, which puts a single pixel right at the center of the circle.

You set this when editing the layer sizes of the circle model in its properties. A zero-size innermost layer becomes the one pixel in the middle, with your larger rings arranged around it.

This is handy when your physical prop has a center node, such as a spinner or a target shape, so the model in xLights matches your wiring and effects light that center pixel correctly.
