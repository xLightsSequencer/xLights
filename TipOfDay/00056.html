Song Structure Regions

You can now mark up sections of a song directly on the timeline. Think Intro, Verse, Chorus, Bridge, Outro ... each one named, colored, and visible the whole time you are sequencing.

You can create regions by hand, or generate them from existing timing marks if you already have a timing track laid out for the song structure.

Once you have regions, you get bulk actions across everything inside one: copy it, fill it, or apply a palette to every effect within the region in one go.

You can also export a region (or several) out as a brand new sequence, which is handy if you want to pull just the chorus out to reuse somewhere else, or to send a short clip to someone for feedback.
