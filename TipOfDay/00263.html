Zip Sequences For ESPixelStick Upload

xLights can package your sequences into a zip for upload to current ESPixelStick controllers.

When you upload a sequence to an ESPixelStick, xLights bundles the files into the zip format the latest firmware expects, so the controller receives everything it needs in one transfer.

This keeps the upload process simple and reliable, without you having to compress or arrange the files yourself before sending them to the device.
