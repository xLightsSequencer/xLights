Edit Your Layout In 3D Mode

The layout preview lets you move and adjust models while you are in 3D mode, not just in the flat 2D view.

Switch the preview to 3D using the toggle on the Layout tab, then select and reposition your models right there in the three-dimensional scene. You can arrange things by depth and height the same way you would in 2D.

This makes a real difference for displays that are not flat, such as trees, arches, and props placed at different distances, because you can see and edit how everything sits in space as you go.
