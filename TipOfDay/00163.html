Channel Count On FPP Connect

The FPP Connect dialog now shows the channel count for your sequences.

When you open FPP Connect to upload, you can see how many channels each sequence uses right there in the list. There is no need to dig into the sequence elsewhere just to know its size before you send it to a controller.

Seeing the channel count up front helps you catch a sequence that is unexpectedly large or small and makes sure you are uploading what you intended.
