Drag Shaders Onto The Grid

You can drop a fragment shader file straight from your file explorer or finder onto the sequencer grid. xLights creates a Shader effect there already pointing at that file.

Grab a .fs shader and drag it onto the model and time range you want, the same way you would drop a video or image. No need to add a blank Shader effect first and then browse for the file.

It's a fast way to audition the many shaders floating around the community, just drop one in, render, and see how it looks on your prop.
