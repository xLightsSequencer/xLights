Auto Wiring For Custom Models

The custom model editor can fill in node numbers for you automatically instead of typing each one by hand.

Right-click in the grid and you'll find auto wiring options that lay the numbers down in a chosen direction, following the usual horizontal or vertical zig-zag path through your cells.

For a large or oddly shaped custom prop this turns a tedious, error-prone job into a couple of clicks, getting the node order right so the model lights in the correct sequence.
