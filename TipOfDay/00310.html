Split An Effect At Your Mouse

When you split an effect, it now breaks at the point where your mouse is pointing rather than always at the center. Hover where you want the cut to happen and use the split command from the right-click menu.

This gives you direct control over where the two halves meet, so you can put the break right on a beat or a moment in the music.

It makes splitting feel much more precise, since you place the cut by eye instead of splitting in the middle and then dragging the edge to fix it.
