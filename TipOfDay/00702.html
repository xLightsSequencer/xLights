Create A Timing Track From Effects

If you have a row of effects whose start and end points line up with the beats or sections you care about, you can turn those positions straight into a timing track. Select the effects, right-click, and choose to create a timing track from them.

xLights drops a timing mark at the start and end of each effect, giving you a fresh timing track that matches the rhythm you already worked out.

It is a quick way to reuse the effort you put into placing effects. Once the marks exist you can snap other effects to them, making the rest of your sequence tighter and easier to build.
