Add A Timing Track To All Views

A popup menu option lets you add a timing track to all of your views at once.

Right-click a timing track and choose the option to add it to every view, rather than opening each view and adding it one at a time. The timing track then shows up wherever you switch views while sequencing.

If you rely on the same beat or bar markers no matter which view you're working in, this keeps them available everywhere without repetitive setup.
