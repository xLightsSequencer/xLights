Scale Imported Models To Your Layout

Models brought in from another layout can now be scaled to match the real-world size of your own layout. When the source layout has a ruler defined, xLights uses it to resize the incoming models to fit the destination.

This happens as part of the import-from-layout process, so a prop that was sized against the other show's ruler arrives at the right physical scale in yours rather than coming in too big or too small.

It saves the tedious step of hand-resizing every imported model, and it keeps spacing and proportions believable when you combine props from different layouts into one display.
