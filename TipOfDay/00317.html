Animate Models In The Preview

The model preview can animate certain models so you can see them move rather than sitting in a fixed pose. This brings models like moving heads and other motion-capable props to life right in the viewer.

Seeing the animation helps you confirm that a fixture is set up correctly and that its range of motion looks the way you intended before you commit to sequencing it.

It is a useful sanity check when configuring a new prop, since a moving preview reveals problems that a still image would hide.
