Bulk Rotate On The Layout Tab

The Layout tab right-click menu now includes Bulk Edit options for rotation on the X, Y, and Z axes.

Select several models, right-click, and choose the axis you want to rotate. You enter one value and it is applied to every selected model at once, so you do not have to open each model and edit its rotation by hand.

This makes it much easier to tilt or spin a whole section of your display together, for example angling a bank of props to face the audience or standing a set of flat models upright.
