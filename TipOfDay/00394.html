Duplicate An Effect

You can duplicate an effect directly, making an exact copy with all of its settings.

Right-click an effect on the grid and choose to duplicate it. The copy carries over every setting from the original, so you get an identical effect to place where you need it without the separate copy and paste steps.

It is handy when you want several instances of the same carefully tuned effect. Duplicate it, then drag the copy where it belongs and adjust only what needs to differ.
