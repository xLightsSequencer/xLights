Export Models For 3D And CAD

You can export your layout, a model, or its wiring to file formats that other programs understand. A DXF file opens in CAD tools, while STL and VRML files describe the model as 3D geometry.

Look for the export options on the Layout tab and on a model's right-click menu. Pick the format that matches what you want to do with it next.

This is handy when you want to print a prop on a 3D printer, plan a physical build in CAD, or hand a wiring diagram to someone helping you put the display together.
