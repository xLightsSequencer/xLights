Estimate Port Current Draw

The controller visualizer can show an estimate of the current draw on a port in its tooltip.

Hover over a port in the visualizer and the tooltip includes an estimate of how much current the pixels on that port could pull. It is based on the number of pixels assigned to the port.

This is a useful sanity check when planning power injection and fusing. Spotting a heavily loaded port before the season starts can save you from dim ends and tripped fuses later.
