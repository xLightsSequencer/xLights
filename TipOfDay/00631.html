Choose Groups When Exporting A Model

When you export a model as an xmodel, xLights lets you decide which of the groups that model belongs to should be included in the file.

A prompt appears during the export so you can tick the groups you want to carry along and leave out the ones you do not. The exported file then only contains the group definitions you chose.

This keeps shared models tidy, so the person importing your prop gets the relevant groups without inheriting a pile of unrelated groups from your layout.
