Layer Your Arches

The Arches model has an option to layer its arches. Instead of laying every arch side by side, you can stack them so they sit one in front of another in the preview.

You will find the setting in the arches model's properties. Layering is the right choice when your real arches are nested or concentric rather than spread across the yard in a row.

Setting this to match how your arches are physically arranged makes the preview look correct and helps effects that run across the whole model behave the way you expect on the actual props.
