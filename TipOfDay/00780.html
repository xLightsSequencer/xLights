Edit Your Key Bindings

xLights lets you assign your own keyboard shortcuts, and you can edit the whole list in one place. Open the preferences and look for the Key Bindings section to see every action you can bind.

From there you can attach a key to an effect so that pressing it drops that effect onto the grid, or change the shortcuts for common commands to keys that feel natural to you. Effect bindings are also what feed quick-access tools like the radial effect wheel.

Taking a few minutes to set up the keys you use most can dramatically speed up sequencing, since the actions you repeat hundreds of times become a single keystroke.
