Model Dimensions In Real World Units

You can edit a model's size using real world dimensions instead of working only in abstract preview units.

In the model's properties you can enter how big it actually is, and xLights scales it to match. This keeps the relative sizes of your props honest against each other in the preview.

Sizing things to their true measurements makes your layout look like your real display, which matters when you are lining up props or judging how an effect will read across the whole yard.
