Bigger Color Buttons

The color selection buttons on the Color panel can now be shown at a larger size.

Look for the size option on the Color panel and choose a bigger button. The swatches grow so the colors in your palette are easier to see and easier to click, which helps a lot on high resolution displays where the standard buttons can feel tiny.

If you spend a lot of time tweaking palettes, larger buttons make picking and comparing colors quicker and less fiddly.
