Renumber DMX Channels In The Visualizer

In the controller visualizer you can renumber the DMX channel of a prop and have the change cascade to the rest of the models on that run.

Right-click a DMX prop in the visualizer and set its starting channel. The models that follow it are shifted to fit, so you do not have to update every fixture by hand after inserting or moving one.

This keeps a chain of DMX fixtures correctly numbered when your wiring changes. Adjust one prop and the rest fall into line automatically.
