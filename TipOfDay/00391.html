A Halloween Palette

xLights includes a ready-made Halloween palette built around orange and purple.

The colors in it were chosen to look good on real pixels without you having to fiddle with gamma to keep them from washing out. Pick it from the palette presets on the Colors panel and your effect takes on a seasonal look instantly.

It is a quick starting point for spooky sequences. Use it as is, or tweak a swatch or two once you see how it renders on your display.
