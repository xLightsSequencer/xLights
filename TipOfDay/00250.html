Close Gaps Between Effects

When you have a row of effects with empty space between them, you can pull them together without dragging each one by hand.

Right-click on the effect grid and pick the option to close the gaps between effects. The effects shift over to sit back to back, removing the dead time that was sitting between them.

It's a quick way to tidy up a row after you have deleted or moved effects around, especially when you want a continuous run of effects with no breaks.
