Replay Without Pressing Play First

The replay button now works on its own, without needing to press play first.

You can jump straight to replaying a section of your sequence with a single click, instead of having to start playback and then trigger the replay. It's right there on the playback controls ready to use.

This saves a step when you're iterating on a small part of a song and want to hear and watch the same stretch over and over while you fine tune your effects.
