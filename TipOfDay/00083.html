Repick Missing Media

When a shader, image, or other media file referenced by an effect can no longer be found, xLights now lets you point it at the correct file instead of leaving it broken.

If a file has moved or was renamed, you get the chance to repick it and the effect picks up the new location. If a shader fails to compile, you are warned about it rather than left wondering why nothing shows up.

This saves you from hunting through effects one by one after reorganizing your media folders or opening a sequence on a different computer.
