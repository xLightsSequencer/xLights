Word Flip Text Movement

The Text effect includes a word flip movement that cycles through your text one word at a time, flipping from one to the next in place rather than scrolling across the model.

Choose it from the movement options in the Text effect. Put each word on its own line, or separate them as the effect expects, and they take turns appearing.

It is a punchy way to deliver short phrases or call-outs on a matrix, especially when a scrolling line would move too fast to read.
