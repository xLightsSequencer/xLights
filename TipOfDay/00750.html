Save FPP Passwords

If your Falcon Player instances are protected by a password, xLights can remember it for you.

When you connect to a password-protected FPP and choose to save the password, xLights stores it so future connections, discoveries, and uploads don't stop to ask again.

This keeps FPP Connect and discovery running smoothly on a secured network, so a password prompt no longer interrupts you every time you push files to your players.
