JPG Images Respect Orientation

When you use a JPG photo in an effect, xLights now reads the orientation tag stored in the image.

Photos taken on phones and cameras often record whether they were held upright or sideways instead of physically rotating the pixels. xLights now honors that tag, so a picture that looked correct on your camera shows up the right way around in the Pictures effect.

This saves you from having to rotate or re-export images by hand before bringing them into a sequence.
