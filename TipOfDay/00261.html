Import Effects As Per Model

When importing effects from another sequence, you can convert them so each model gets its own copy of the effects.

Look for the option to convert to Per Model during the import mapping step. Instead of effects rendering across a whole group as one buffer, every model in the group receives the effects individually.

This is useful when you want each prop to play the effect on its own rather than sharing one combined render, giving you finer control after the import is done.
