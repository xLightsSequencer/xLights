Align Effects To The Closest Timing Mark

When you have effects that start or end just a little off from your timing track, you can snap them into place. Select the effects you want to tidy up, right-click, and look for the option to align them to the closest timing mark.

Each selected effect moves its edges to the nearest mark on the active timing track, so a whole row of slightly-misplaced effects lines up with the beat in one step.

This is a quick way to clean up effects that were dragged by hand, and it keeps your transitions landing exactly on the music instead of a few frames early or late.
