Reorder Your Presets

You can rearrange the effect presets in your preset panel rather than living with the order they were saved in. Right-click in the preset list for sorting options, and use the buttons to move a preset up, down, to the top, or to the bottom.

This lets you put your most-used presets where you can reach them quickly and keep related ones grouped together. Over a season your preset list grows, so a tidy order pays off.

Arranging presets the way you think about your show makes dropping them onto models faster, since you are not scrolling past everything to find the one you want.
