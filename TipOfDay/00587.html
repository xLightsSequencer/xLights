Color Filter On The Effects Panel

The effect select panel, where you pick which effects to drag onto the grid, has a color filter. It lets you narrow the list down so you only see what you are looking for.

Use the filter at the top of the panel to cut through the full set of effects. It is especially helpful while you are learning the effects, since you do not have to scroll past everything every time.

Combined with the panel's other filtering, it makes finding the effect you want quicker, keeping your focus on sequencing instead of hunting through a long list.
