Generate Palettes With Local AI Using Ollama

When generating a color palette with AI, you can choose which service provider does the work. One of the options is Ollama, which runs an AI model locally on your own computer.

Pick Ollama as the provider in the Generate Palette settings once you have it installed and running. Your palette requests are then handled on your machine rather than being sent to an online service.

This is useful if you prefer to keep things local, want to work without relying on an internet connection, or simply like having control over which model is used to come up with your colors.
