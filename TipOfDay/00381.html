Shift Your Palette Colors

The color palette on the Colors panel has shift buttons that rotate every color along by one position at once.

Instead of dragging each swatch to a new slot by hand, click the shift button and the whole palette steps left or right. Effects that use the palette redraw with the colors in their new order.

It is a quick way to audition different color arrangements on an effect. If a pattern would look better starting on a different color, one click rotates the set rather than rebuilding the palette from scratch.
