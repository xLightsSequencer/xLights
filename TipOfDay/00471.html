Shimmer On Face Effects

Face effects support a shimmer look, and you can also choose to suppress shimmer on a particular face when you do not want it.

Shimmer adds a rapid on-off flicker, which can give matrix faces extra life. The suppress option lets you keep that flicker out of a specific face effect so a steady mouth or eye stays steady while other parts shimmer.

Having control in both directions means you can use shimmer where it adds energy and turn it off where it would distract, all without changing the rest of your face setup.
