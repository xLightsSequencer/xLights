Choose Your Video Export Codec

When you export a video of your sequence, you can pick which video codec xLights uses to encode it.

The setting lets you choose a codec that suits where the video is headed, whether that is wide compatibility for sharing, smaller file size, or higher quality. Different players and websites prefer different codecs, so picking the right one avoids files that will not play.

Setting the codec yourself means your exported preview videos come out ready to use, without an extra conversion step after the fact.
