Scrub The Preview With Your Audio

As you drag along the timeline, the model preview follows so you see exactly what your display is doing at that instant.

Scrub the playhead across the waveform and the preview updates frame by frame to match the spot you are pointing at. This works on regular sequences as well as animations.

It's a precise way to line up effects with a beat or a lyric, letting you nudge to an exact moment and confirm the lights look right there without having to play the whole section.
