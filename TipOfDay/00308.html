Prepare Audio For Smoother Playback

The Prepare Audio tool converts your song into a clean, consistent format that xLights handles smoothly during sequencing and playback. It re-encodes the file and carries over the metadata and tags from your original so you don't lose the track information.

If a song plays back unevenly or scrubs poorly on the timeline, running it through Prepare Audio often fixes it by giving xLights a well-behaved audio file to work with.

It is worth doing for any track that came from an unusual source, since starting with prepared audio avoids a whole class of playback and timing headaches.
