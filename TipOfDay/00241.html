Find And Replace In Timing Tracks

You can search a timing track for text and replace it, straight from a right click menu on the track.

This is great for lyric tracks where the same word appears many times and you need to correct a spelling, swap a phoneme, or fix a label everywhere it occurs. Instead of clicking through each timing mark by hand, you change them all in one pass.

It also helps when you reuse a timing track between songs and need to update repeated words consistently across the whole track.
