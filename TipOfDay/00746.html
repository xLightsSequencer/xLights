Temporarily Switch Show Folders

You can change your show folder just for the current session without making it your permanent default.

Use the temporary show folder option when you open a folder, and xLights works in that folder for now but returns to your usual one the next time you start up.

It's perfect for taking a quick look at someone else's sequence, helping a friend debug their layout, or testing something in a throwaway folder, all without losing track of your real show.
