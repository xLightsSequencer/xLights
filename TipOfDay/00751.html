See Coordinates While Dragging Models

When you grab a model's handle and drag it on the Layout tab, the current coordinates appear in the status bar at the bottom of the window.

Watch the numbers update as you move so you can place a model at an exact spot, or match its position to another model, without guessing by eye.

It's a small touch that makes precise alignment much easier, especially when you want several props sharing the same row, column, or height in your display.
