Keep Servo Start And End In Sync

The Servo effect panel has a Sync checkbox that keeps the start and end positions matched while you adjust them. With it turned on, changing one value moves the other to stay in step.

This is the easy way to hold a servo at a steady position for the length of an effect, since you only set the value once and both ends follow along.

Turn it off again when you want the servo to travel from one position to another over the course of the effect.
