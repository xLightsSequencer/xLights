Reorder The Model List Columns

The model list on the Layout tab lets you drag its column headings into whatever order you prefer. Grab a heading and move it left or right, and the list rearranges to match.

xLights remembers the order you choose, so it stays that way the next time you open the program. You can put the columns you care about most, like start channel or controller, right where you want them.

It is a small touch that makes a long model list easier to scan, especially once you have a lot of props and want the important details up front.
