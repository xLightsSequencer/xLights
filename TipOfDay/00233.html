Import Effects Onto Submodels

When importing selected effects from another sequence, submodels are now handled as part of the import.

Use the import effects dialog and map your source rows to the matching submodels on your models. The effects that were placed on submodels in the original sequence come across to the submodels in your sequence.

This is useful when you reuse work between sequences and your models rely on submodels for detail, since you no longer lose that part of the sequence when bringing effects over.
