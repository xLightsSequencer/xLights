Find Missing Images In Bulk

xLights can now search a sequence for image files that have gone missing and help you locate them all at once.

This is handy when you have moved a show folder, renamed a media directory, or imported a sequence from someone else and several Pictures effects can no longer find their files. Instead of hunting down each broken effect one at a time, you can resolve them together.

Fixing the paths in one pass saves a lot of clicking and makes sure nothing renders black because an image quietly disappeared.
