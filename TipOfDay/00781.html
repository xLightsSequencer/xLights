Suspend And Suppress Effects

You can temporarily turn an effect off without deleting it. Right-click an effect on the grid and you will find options to suspend or suppress it.

A suppressed effect stays on the grid but is skipped when xLights renders, so its model goes dark for that time. This is perfect for auditioning a sequence with one layer removed, or for muting a section while you work on something underneath it.

Because the effect and all its settings are still there, you can re-enable it at any time and pick up exactly where you left off, which beats deleting and rebuilding it later.
