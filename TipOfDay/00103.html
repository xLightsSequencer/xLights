Dock And Float The Layout And Presets Panels

The Layout tab's Models and Properties panels can now be undocked and floated, and the Effect Presets window can be docked into the main window.

Drag a panel by its title bar to pull it loose, drop it where you want it, or float it as a separate window. The Presets window works the other way too, snapping into the main window so it stays in view while you sequence.

This lets you arrange the workspace to suit your screen, especially on a second monitor where you can spread panels out for more room. Your layout is remembered, so it stays the way you set it.
