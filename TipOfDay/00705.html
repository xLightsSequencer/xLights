Remove Word And Phoneme Layers

A lyric timing track can hold up to three layers: phrases, words and phonemes. If you only need the phrase or word level, you can strip the lower layers back out.

Right-click the timing track and choose to remove the word or phoneme layer. The remaining layers stay untouched, so you keep the timing you want and drop the detail you do not.

This keeps lipsync tracks tidy and is useful when an automatic breakdown produced more detail than your sequence needs, or when you want to redo the breakdown from a cleaner starting point.
