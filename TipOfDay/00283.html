Hide The Effect Indicator On Deep Groups

There's a setting to turn off the effect indicator that appears on deeply nested groups.

When a group contains many sub-groups, xLights normally shows an indicator on the collapsed row to tell you effects exist below it. If you find that clutters the grid, you can now disable it in the settings.

Turning it off can make the sequencer grid cleaner to look at when you work with large, layered group hierarchies and don't need the reminder.
