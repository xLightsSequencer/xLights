Color Your Candle Effect

The Candle effect can optionally take its colors from the color palette instead of always using its built-in flame colors.

Turn on the palette option in the Candle effect settings, then set the colors you want on the color panel. The flickering flame is drawn using those colors, so you are no longer limited to the traditional warm candle look.

This opens the door to colored flames for themed displays, such as cool blues for a winter scene or greens for something spooky, while keeping the natural candle flicker.
