Align Models To The Ground

The Layout tab includes alignment commands that snap selected models to the front, to the back, or down to the ground.

Select several models, then use the align options to line them up against a common edge. Aligning to the ground is especially useful in a 3D layout, where it drops everything onto the same base so your props sit level instead of floating at different heights.

You can also assign these alignments to keybindings, making it quick to tidy a group of props into neat rows as you build out your display.
