Hide Other Controllers In The Visualiser

The controller visualiser can get busy when you have many controllers. It lets you hide the models that are assigned to other controllers so you can focus on just the one you are wiring.

With the clutter from unrelated controllers removed, the ports and models for the controller you are working on are much easier to see and drag around. The hidden models are still there, just out of the way.

This keeps the visualiser readable on a large show and reduces the chance of dropping a model onto the wrong controller while you sort out your port assignments.
