Search The Model Download

The vendor model download dialog has a search box at the top. Type part of a name and the list narrows down to matching props as you go.

The catalogue of downloadable models from vendors is large, so scrolling through everything to find one prop is slow. The search box jumps you straight to what you are after.

It makes grabbing a ready-made model quick, whether you are looking for a particular shape, a brand, or just browsing for something to fill a gap in your display.
