Transitions Built For Matrices

Among the in and out transitions are several designed to look their best on matrices and other high-density props. They use the extra pixels to create shapes and patterns a sparse prop could not show.

You set them like any other transition, on the In and Out tabs of an effect, choosing a type and how long it lasts. On a dense grid these reveal and conceal the effect with stars, sweeps and other forms that read clearly.

If you have a P5 panel matrix or a tightly packed prop, it is worth trying these transitions, since shapes that vanish on a single strand of lights come alive when there are enough pixels to draw them.
