A Taller Audio Waveform

The audio waveform at the top of the sequencer can be shown at double height. Right-click the waveform to find the display options.

A taller waveform makes quiet passages and subtle beats much easier to see, which helps when you are placing timing marks or lining effects up to the music by eye. The extra detail does not push the rest of the sequencer down, so you keep your working space.

It is a small touch that pays off on songs with a lot of dynamic range, where the important moments can be hard to pick out in a short waveform.
