Moving Head Pattern Tab

The Moving Head effect has a new Pattern tab that builds pan and tilt movement from parametric shapes such as Circle, Eight, Diamond and Lissajous, modeled on the QLC+ EFX patterns.

Instead of keying pan and tilt by hand, you pick a shape and the effect sweeps your heads through it automatically. The rotation of the pattern is value-curve animatable, so you can spin or tumble the whole figure over the length of the effect.

It is a quick way to get smooth, organic movement out of a row of moving heads without drawing every position yourself.
