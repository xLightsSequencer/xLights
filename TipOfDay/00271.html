Trim Audio In Sequence Settings

You can now trim the audio attached to a sequence directly from the sequence settings.

Open the sequence settings and look for the audio trim option. It lets you cut unwanted time off the start or end of your song without having to edit the file in a separate audio editor first.

This is useful when your music has a long silent intro or trailing dead air, so your timing marks and effects line up cleanly with the part of the track you actually want to use.
