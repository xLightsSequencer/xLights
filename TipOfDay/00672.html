Mirror And Copy In The Warp Effect

The Warp effect includes copy and mirror options that repeat the warped image across the model.

Add a Warp effect over another effect or image and look in its settings for the copy and mirror controls. They tile or reflect the warped result, turning a single distortion into a symmetrical, repeating pattern.

This is an easy way to get kaleidoscope-like symmetry from the warp you already have. Mirroring keeps both halves matched, which often looks more deliberate on a tree or matrix.
