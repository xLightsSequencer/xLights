Upload To Inactive Controllers

You can upload configuration to a controller even when it is marked inactive on the Setup tab.

Normally an inactive controller is skipped during output, but there are times you still want to push its settings, such as preparing a board you have not wired in yet or staging a spare. xLights now lets the upload go ahead rather than forcing you to activate it first.

This makes it easier to get controllers ready ahead of time. Configure and upload them while they sit inactive, then activate them only when they are actually part of your running show.
