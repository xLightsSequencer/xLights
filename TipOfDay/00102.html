Generate A Timing Track From The Beat

The "Add Timing Track" dialog has an "Audio Tempo" option that detects your song's tempo and drops a timing track with a mark on every beat.

Pick it from the list of timing-track types, confirm the detected BPM, and xLights fills the track with back-to-back marks aligned to the beat across the whole song.

It gives you an instant rhythmic grid to sequence against, which is great for getting effects to land on the beat. Look in the same dialog for related options like Audio Chords and Audio Onsets that pull other musical features out of your track.
