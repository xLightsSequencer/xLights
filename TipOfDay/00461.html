Flip A Custom Model Selection

When you are editing a custom model, you can select a region of cells and flip it horizontally or vertically right inside the Custom Model dialog.

Highlight the area you want, then use the flip options to mirror just that selection. This is much faster than re-typing node numbers when one half of a prop should mirror the other, or when you realise a section was laid out the wrong way round.

It makes building symmetrical props and fixing mistakes far less tedious, since you can reuse work you have already done instead of starting a section over.
