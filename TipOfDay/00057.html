Radial Effect Wheel

Double-click on an empty part of the sequencer grid (not on a timing track) and a small radial wheel pops up around your cursor.

It gives you quick access to your effect keybindings without having to reach over to the effects panel or remember the keyboard shortcut. Pick the slice you want and it drops straight onto the grid at that point.

It's a nice shortcut once you have effect keybindings set up, since it puts all of them one click away no matter where you are on the timeline.
