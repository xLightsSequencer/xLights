Manage Smart Remotes In The Visualizer

The controller visualizer shows smart receivers as a visible part of the layout, and you can rearrange them right there.

Right-click a smart remote to quickly change its number, which moves all the models on it at once, and you can drag and drop remotes to reorganize them. Tooltips tell you what each one is as you hover.

Handling smart receivers visually makes it much easier to see how your strings are grouped and to fix a mis-numbered remote, instead of editing each model's settings separately.
