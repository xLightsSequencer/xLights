Search Sequence Downloads By Creator

When you browse the sequence downloads inside xLights, the search now looks at the creator as well as the song and title. Type a sequence author's name and the list filters to their work.

This is useful when you have found a creator whose style you like and want to see everything of theirs that is available to download, rather than hunting song by song.

It also helps when a friend recommends a particular sequence maker. Searching their name brings up their catalogue so you can grab a starting point and adapt it for your own display.
