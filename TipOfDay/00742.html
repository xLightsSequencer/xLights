Erase Existing Effects On Import

When you import effects from another sequence, you can choose to erase any effects already sitting on the layers you are importing onto.

Look for the option in the import mapping dialog. With it turned on, the incoming effects replace whatever was on those target layers rather than landing on top of and mixing with them.

It's the cleaner choice when you are bringing in a finished pass and want it to be exactly what plays, with no leftover effects from an earlier attempt blending through.
