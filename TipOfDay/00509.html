Colour Search And Replace

The Edit menu has a colour search and replace function that swaps one colour for another across your effects.

Tell it the colour to look for and the colour to use instead, and it updates the matching colours in your sequence in one pass instead of editing every effect's palette by hand.

It is the fast way to retheme a sequence, fix a colour that does not show well on your pixels, or nudge a shade to better match the rest of your show.
