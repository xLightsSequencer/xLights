Lua Scripting For Sequence Import

If you regularly import sequences and find the automatic model mapping never quite matches your naming conventions, you can now write a Lua script to drive the mapping yourself.

This is aimed at people comfortable with a bit of scripting who import a lot of sequences from other shows or other sequencers and want full control over how source models get matched to your models, rather than relying on name-based auto-mapping alone.

It's not something most users will need, but if you've ever wished you could just write the matching rule instead of fixing up the mapping by hand every time, this is for you.
