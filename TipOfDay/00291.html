Export And Import Icicle Models

Icicle models can now be exported and imported directly.

On the Layout tab, right-click an icicle model to export it to a file, or import one that you or someone else saved earlier. The model comes in with its settings intact, ready to position in your layout.

This makes it easy to reuse an icicle setup across shows, or to share a tested model with others instead of recreating all the spacing and drop settings by hand.
