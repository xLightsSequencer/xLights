Generate Images With Gemini

You can now use Google's Gemini to generate images from a text description, right inside xLights.

Once you have entered your Gemini key in the AI settings, you can describe the picture you want and let it create one for you instead of hunting for artwork to drop onto a model. It works alongside the other image generation options, so you can pick whichever service you prefer.

This is handy when you need a quick custom image for a Pictures effect or a matrix display and don't already have something on hand.
