Search Effect Settings

With so many effects, each with its own pile of options, it can be hard to remember which panel holds the setting you want. The effect settings search panel finds it for you.

Type a word or two and xLights lists the matching settings from across the effect panels, so you can jump straight to the control instead of clicking through tabs hunting for it.

It is a quick way to learn what an effect can do, and to reach a rarely-used option without remembering exactly where it lives.
