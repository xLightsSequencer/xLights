Center And Stop Repeating Text

The Text effect can center your words and show them just once instead of tiling them. When you use one of the built-in XLFonts, look for the Center option and the No Repeat option in the effect's settings.

Centering keeps your message neatly in the middle of the model, and No Repeat stops the text from being copied over and over to fill the buffer, which is what you want for a single clean line.

Together they make it much easier to place a short word or number exactly where you want it on a matrix or sign.
