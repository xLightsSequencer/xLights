Accidental Resize Protection

When you click an effect on the grid to select it, xLights now guards against tiny mouse movements being treated as a drag of the effect's edge.

Before, a single careless click near the start or end of an effect could nudge its timing by a frame or two without you noticing. Now a plain click just selects the effect, and you have to make a clear, deliberate move on the edge to actually resize it.

This makes selecting effects feel a lot safer, especially on a trackpad or when you are zoomed out and the effects are small. Your existing timings stay put unless you really mean to change them.
