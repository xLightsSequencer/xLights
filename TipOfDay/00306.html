Flip The Warp Effect

The Warp effect has a Flip option that mirrors how the warp is applied. You'll find it among the Warp effect's settings alongside the warp type and its other controls.

Flipping reverses the direction of the distortion, so a warp that pulls toward one side instead pulls toward the other. It lets you get a mirrored look without rebuilding the effect from scratch.

It is handy when you want two props to warp in opposite directions, giving a symmetric, mirror-image feel across your display.
