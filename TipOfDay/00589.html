Join Submodels

If you have built several submodels and want them combined, xLights can join them into one. Instead of recreating the node ranges by hand, you pick the submodels you want and merge them together.

You will find this in the submodels dialog for a model, where your submodels are listed. Joining takes the nodes from the pieces and rolls them into a single submodel.

This is useful when you started out with lots of small submodels and later realise you want to treat them as one section, saving you from rebuilding the combined range from scratch.
