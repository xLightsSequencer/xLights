Keep Group Membership When Copying Models

When you copy and paste a model, xLights can add the new copy to the same groups the original belonged to. There is an option to keep that group membership instead of leaving the new model out of every group.

This is a real time-saver when you duplicate a prop that needs to behave like its sibling. The copy lands in your "all lights", mega-tree, or whatever groups the original was part of, with no extra clicking.

Without it you would have to open each group and add the new model by hand. Letting the paste carry the memberships across keeps your groups complete as your layout grows.
