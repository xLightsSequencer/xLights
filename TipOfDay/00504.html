Silence The Audio

xLights has an option to mute its audio, so you can keep working on a sequence without the music playing every time you press play.

This is a lifesaver when you are sequencing late at night, working in a shared space, or just need to watch the visual preview closely without the soundtrack repeating in your ears.

Toggle it off again whenever you want to line effects up to the beat by ear. Your sequence and its timing are unaffected either way.
