Sort Your Controllers

The Controllers tab now gives you options for sorting the list of controllers you have set up.

Once you have more than a handful of controllers, the order they were added in stops being helpful. The sort options let you reorder the list so it is easier to find the one you are looking for and to keep related controllers grouped together.

This is handy on larger shows where scrolling through a long, unordered list to find a single controller wastes time.
