Twinkly Controller Support

xLights supports Twinkly controllers, including discovering them on your network so you do not have to enter their details by hand.

Run a controller discovery and any Twinkly devices it finds can be added to your setup. You can also import a Twinkly device's own pixel layout into a custom model, so your preview matches how the lights are actually mapped.

That makes it straightforward to drive consumer Twinkly strings and mapped lights from the same sequence as the rest of your show.
