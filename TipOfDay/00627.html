Export A Submodel As An xmodel

You can export a submodel on its own as an xmodel file, capturing just that group of nodes and its definition.

Find the export option on the submodel in the model's properties. This is useful when you need to renumber a model's nodes, since renumbering normally breaks the submodels that point at the old node numbers. Export the submodels first, renumber, then bring them back.

It also lets you reuse a carefully built submodel on another model, so you do not have to redraw the same node selection from scratch.
