Import Color Palettes

You can import color palettes so that a set of colors you like is ready to reuse across your sequences.

Bring in palettes from the color panel and they join your saved palettes, ready to apply to any effect with a click. This makes it easy to share a consistent color scheme between sequences or pick up palettes shared by others in the community.

If you have spent time getting just the right combination of colors, importing palettes means you set them up once and use them everywhere instead of rebuilding them by hand.
