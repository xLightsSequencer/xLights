Convert A Custom Model To Single Channel

The custom model data grid has a right-click option to convert a pixel (RGB) model into a single channel model.

Open a custom model's data, right-click in the grid, and choose the option to convert it to single channel. This is the quick way to turn a model built for RGB pixels into one that drives plain on/off or single-colour channels.

It saves rebuilding the model from scratch when you realise the hardware behind it is single channel rather than full colour. The layout you already drew is kept, just with a different channel type.
