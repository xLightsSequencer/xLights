KiNet Uploads Through FPP

If you drive lighting over the KiNet protocol, xLights can include KiNet output universes when it uploads your output configuration to an FPP device.

Set up your KiNet outputs as normal, then use FPP Connect to push the configuration. The KiNet universes are sent along with the rest, so the player knows how to reach those fixtures.

This lets FPP act as the output for KiNet hardware in your show, keeping all of your channel definitions managed in one place in xLights.
