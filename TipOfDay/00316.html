Disable Monitoring For A Controller

You can turn off monitoring by default for individual controllers. In the controller's settings there is an option that stops xLights from continually checking on that device.

This is helpful for controllers that aren't always connected, or that live on a different network, where the repeated checks would otherwise produce errors or slow things down.

Leaving monitoring on for your everyday controllers and off for the occasional ones keeps your status display clean and your startup quick.
