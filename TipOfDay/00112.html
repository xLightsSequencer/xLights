Convert Videos Now

When you load a sequence, xLights may warn you that some video files are in a format that does not play back well. That warning now includes a "Convert Videos Now" button.

Click it and xLights transcodes the flagged videos to a compatible .mov file saved next to the originals, then updates the matching video effects in your sequence to point at the converted files for you.

This takes the guesswork out of fixing problem media. Rather than re-encoding files by hand in another tool and re-linking each effect, you let xLights handle the conversion and the filename updates in one step.
