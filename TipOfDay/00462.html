Reverse Fades In Color Wash

The Color Wash effect has a Reverse Fades option that flips the direction of its fade in and fade out behaviour.

You will find it on the Color Wash settings panel alongside the other wash controls. Turning it on swaps when the wash builds up and when it falls away, giving you a different feel without changing your colours or timing.

It is an easy way to get variety out of an effect you probably already use a lot, and to make a wash line up better with the mood of a particular part of the song.
